About Cal-Therm Corporation

Over 55 years of mechanical insulation expertise, built on a foundation of quality craftsmanship, deep industry knowledge, and an unwavering commitment to our clients.

Who We Are

Trusted Expertise Since 1969

Cal-Therm Insulation was formed in 1969 by Jim Smiles, a veteran of the insulation industry in California. The company has been built on a foundation of quality craftsmanship and deep industry expertise spanning over five decades.

Today, under the leadership of Brian Campbell, who joined the company in 1986 and became principal owner in 2011, Cal-Therm continues its tradition of excellence. Brian began his career in industrial insulation in 1968 and has worked on every type of project setting and in several different countries.
