Professional HVAC Duct Insulation Services

Properly insulated HVAC duct systems are essential for energy efficiency, occupant comfort, and indoor air quality. Uninsulated or poorly insulated ductwork can result in significant energy losses, condensation problems, and excessive noise transmission throughout a building. At Cal-Therm, we specialize in insulating duct systems for commercial, institutional, and industrial facilities.

Our HVAC duct insulation services address every aspect of ductwork performance. We reduce thermal losses in supply and return air ducts, prevent condensation on cold surfaces, minimize noise transmission between spaces, and help maintain optimal indoor air quality. Whether you are constructing a new building or upgrading an existing system, our experienced crews deliver precision installations that meet all applicable codes and specifications.

Applications

  • Commercial office buildings
  • Retail and shopping centers
  • Healthcare facilities
  • Educational institutions
  • Industrial ventilation systems
  • Clean room environments
  • Restaurant and kitchen exhaust

Our Approach

Every HVAC duct insulation project begins with a detailed evaluation of the ductwork layout, operating conditions, and building requirements. We select the right insulation materials, vapor barriers, and jacketing systems to ensure long-term thermal performance, condensation control, and acoustic attenuation. Our skilled installers understand the importance of continuous coverage, sealed joints, and proper support to deliver systems that perform reliably for years to come.
