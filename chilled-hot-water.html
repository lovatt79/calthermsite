Expert Chilled & Hot Water Insulation Services

Chilled and hot water piping systems are the backbone of comfort and process cooling in commercial and institutional buildings. Without proper insulation, these systems suffer significant energy losses, condensation problems, and reduced performance. Cal-Therm specializes in insulating chilled and hot water distribution systems to maximize energy efficiency and protect your infrastructure.

Our insulation systems prevent condensation on chilled water lines, minimize heat loss on hot water piping, and maintain precise delivery temperatures throughout your facility. We serve commercial buildings, hospitals, universities, data centers, and district energy systems across California and Arizona.

Applications

  • Commercial building HVAC systems
  • Hospital and healthcare facilities
  • University campus systems
  • Data center cooling
  • District energy systems
  • Hotel and resort facilities
  • Industrial cooling systems

Our Approach

Every chilled and hot water insulation project begins with a comprehensive evaluation of your piping systems, operating temperatures, and facility requirements. We select the right insulation materials and vapor retarder systems to eliminate condensation on cold lines and reduce heat loss on hot water piping. Our skilled crews deliver clean, code-compliant installations that lower your energy costs and extend the life of your mechanical systems.
