Professional insulation solutions for commercial and industrial refrigeration systems, ensuring energy efficiency, condensation control, and reliable cold chain performance.

Expert Refrigeration Insulation Services

Commercial and industrial refrigeration systems demand high-performance insulation to maintain consistent temperatures, prevent condensation, and reduce energy costs. At Cal-Therm, we bring decades of experience insulating cold storage facilities, food processing plants, ammonia refrigeration systems, and walk-in coolers and freezers.

Our refrigeration insulation systems are engineered to minimize thermal transfer, control moisture, and protect your equipment from corrosion. We work with facility owners, general contractors, and mechanical engineers to deliver insulation solutions that meet strict food safety, building code, and energy efficiency requirements.

Applications

  • Cold storage warehouses and distribution centers
  • Food processing plants
  • Ammonia refrigeration systems
  • Walk-in coolers and freezers
  • Supermarket refrigeration systems
  • Beverage cooling systems
  • Pharmaceutical cold chain facilities

Our Approach

Every refrigeration insulation project begins with a comprehensive evaluation of your system layout, operating temperatures, and facility requirements. We select the right insulation materials and vapor retarder systems to prevent moisture ingress and ensure long-term thermal performance. Our skilled crews deliver precise, code-compliant installations that keep your refrigeration systems running efficiently and your products at the correct temperature.

Refrigeration Cold Storage California

Cold Storage Distribution Center

Full-scale refrigeration insulation for a 200,000 sq. ft. cold storage distribution center, including ammonia piping, evaporator rooms, and dock areas. The project required careful coordination with ongoing operations to maintain temperature integrity throughout the installation process.

Refrigeration Food Processing Arizona

Food Processing Plant Retrofit

Complete insulation replacement for an aging food processing facility, upgrading all refrigeration piping, walk-in freezers, and blast chillers. The retrofit improved energy efficiency by reducing thermal losses and eliminating persistent condensation issues throughout the plant.
