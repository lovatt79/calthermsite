Cryogenic Insulation

Specialized insulation solutions for ultra-low temperature systems, protecting critical cryogenic infrastructure from heat gain, condensation, and ice formation.

Expert Cryogenic Insulation Services

Cryogenic systems operate at extremely low temperatures, often below -150°F, and require specialized insulation to maintain thermal efficiency and prevent dangerous condensation and ice buildup. At Cal-Therm, we have decades of experience insulating cryogenic piping, vessels, and equipment.

Our cryogenic insulation systems are designed to minimize heat gain, prevent moisture infiltration, and maintain the integrity of your low-temperature processes. We work with LNG facilities, air separation plants, food processing operations, and research laboratories.

Applications

  • Liquid Natural Gas (LNG) piping and storage
  • Liquid nitrogen and oxygen systems
  • Air separation plants
  • Cold storage and food processing facilities
  • Pharmaceutical and biotech cryogenic equipment
  • Research laboratory systems
  • Liquid hydrogen and helium systems

Our Approach

Every cryogenic insulation project starts with a thorough assessment of your system requirements. We select the appropriate insulation materials and vapor barrier systems to ensure long-term performance in the most demanding low-temperature environments. Our experienced crews understand the critical nature of these installations and deliver precision workmanship on every project.

LNG Terminal Pipe Insulation
